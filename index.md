## **About Me**

I am a bioinformatics scientist with expertise in pathogen genomics, high-throughput sequencing, and public health surveillance. My work spans whole-genome sequencing, metagenomics, transcriptomics, and outbreak-driven genomic surveillance across Africa and the UK. I have extensive experience designing pipelines, leading sequencing teams, coordinating field and laboratory operations, and delivering hands-on training for international programs.

I currently work at the **Centre for Virus Research (University of Glasgow)**, where I develop pipelines, support HTS workflows, optimize NGS protocols, and contribute to research, publication, and grant efforts.

Previously, I led the **Sequencing Facility at KEMRI-Wellcome Trust**, managing SARS-CoV-2 sequencing for national response, developing diagnostics, coordinating genomic surveillance, and delivering large-scale bioinformatics training for Africa CDC and Chan Zuckerberg Biohub.
