LOCUS PROFILE  ·  scientific summary

What I do

My work spans genomics, transcriptomics, metatranscriptomics and pathogen genomics. I develop reproducible computational workflows, integrate diverse omics datasets, and translate sequencing data into biological discoveries that advance our understanding of disease mechanisms, biomarker discovery and precision medicine. I thrive in collaborative, multidisciplinary research environments where computational innovation drives biological and clinical discovery.
